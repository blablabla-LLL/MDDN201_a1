ABOUT

With the development of e-commerce, online retail has one of the components of e-commerce, which has also developed rapidly. The emergence of the online retail industry allows people to enjoy the convenience of buying goods without leaving their homes, and at the same time, it also brings new means of profit for businesses.

Definition of online shopping.

Know more about the FACT about online shopping.




AIM

Online shopping aims to let everyone know more about online shopping and the pros and cons of online consumption. With the development of information technology and the popularization of the Internet, people's lifestyles, travel methods, shopping methods, Etc., are undergoing earth-shaking changes. For example, people choose to order a taxi online to reduce their waiting time; people choose online trading platforms such as Taobao and Amazon to make their lives easier. The development of information technology and the rapid spread of the Internet have aroused many people's research and attention.




TARGET

Compared with the traditional retail industry, online shopping has its own advantages and disadvantages. Its advantages are obvious to all of our consumers, and its disadvantages are obvious. Since its inception, online shopping has undergone rapid development and changes in the direction of self-improvement. . For the majority of consumers when using the two purchasing methods, they must analyse their purchasing situation to choose the purchasing method that is more suitable for them.

Typography

Text is the most intuitive product information, and any expression is not as accurate as text. As a shopping website, I want the website to be more concise and intuitive. My main font color is black, and the "Aparajita" font editing is light and has a rhythm of thickness changes.




Color Palette

Regarding the colors of the website, I chose 6 colors. Regarding the choice of colors, I prefer the brighter colors. So for this website, I used contrasting colors, orange, yellow and green to highlight the characteristics of the website, which can also impress new users in a short time.I want to use color blocks as the guide in each area, and use bright colors to guide the visual center of gravity. The main group of online consumption is women, and the pages are mainly in warm colors, creating a lively, warm and secure visual psychology. The background color of the webpage is white. White is acolorless system. It can be matched with any color. It is very reasonable to use as a background color assistance. It naturally creates a clear theme, comfortable and harmonious visual experience, and makes users deeply integrated into the website. In the context of using various color blocks and multiple visual levels, visually stimulate users’ desire to shop.

Fact about online shopping

Because it is searched on the website, one of the main factors influencing netizens to purchase online goods is the evaluation of other netizens on the product. Especially when you want to buy products with relatively high prices, netizens need information channels and platforms that can truly understand the actual situation of the products. Only when consumers communicate with consumers will their consumption vigilance be minimized, or even directly reduced Unconscious consumption is transformed into active consumption. At this time, the characteristics of "interactivity", "immediateness" and "convenience" precisely meet the information sharing needs of netizens.

Definition of online shopping

Online shopping is to retrieve product information through the Internet and send a shopping request through an electronic order form, and then fill in a personal checking account or credit card number. The manufacturer will ship the goods by mail order or deliver the goods to the door through the courier. For online shopping, the general payment method is payment on delivery (direct bank transfer, online remittance), and the guaranteed transaction is cash on delivery, Etc.
